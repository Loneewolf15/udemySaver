How to get your Access Token

  1. Open a new tab and go to udemy.com.
  2. Log in to your Udemy account normally.
  3. Press F12 (or Right-Click → Inspect) to open Developer Tools.
  4. Go to the Application tab (in Chrome/Edge) or Storage tab (in Firefox).
  5. On the left sidebar, expand Cookies and click on https://www.udemy.com.
  6. Search for the name access_token in the list.
  7. Double-click its Value, copy it, and paste it here!
